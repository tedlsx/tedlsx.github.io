Introduction of exponential family

The exponential family

p(y;η)=b(y)exp(ηTT(y)−α(η))p(y;\eta) = b(y) exp(\eta^T T(y) - \alpha(\eta)) p(y;η)=b(y)exp(ηTT(y)−α(η))

  • η\etaη is natural parameter
  • T(y)T(y)T(y) is sufficient statistic
